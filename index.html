Custom Blending Guide

All Bronsun shades can be mixed together before adding developer. Mix dye shades first, then add developer.

How to Read a Ratio
Everything is measured in pumps — one pump = one full press of the dye tube nozzle.
Part 1 — Mixing Dye Shades Together
The ratio tells you how many pumps of each shade to use. The first number is the dominant shade, the second is the accent shade. Always mix your dye shades together before adding any developer.
3:1 Ratio
#5 Light Brown + #2 Graphite
3 pumps Light Brown
1 pump Graphite
= 4 pumps dye total
More pumps of a shade = more of that color in the final result.
2:1 Ratio
#4.1 Red Chestnut + #4 Chestnut
2 pumps Red Chestnut
1 pump Chestnut
= 3 pumps dye total
Need more product? Scale up but keep the ratio — 4 pumps + 2 pumps is still a 2:1.
1:1 Ratio
#6 Brown + #7 Dark Brown
1 pump Brown
1 pump Dark Brown
= 2 pumps dye total
Equal parts. Neither shade dominates — they blend into one balanced tone.
Part 2 — Adding Developer (Always 1:1)
Once your dye shades are mixed, add developer. Bronsun always uses a strict 1:1 dye-to-developer ratio — whatever your total dye pumps are, match it with the exact same number of developer pumps. This never changes regardless of shade, blend, or hair density.
Single Shade
2 pumps #4 Chestnut (dye)
+ 2 pumps developer
= ready to apply
Blended Shades (3:1)
3 pumps Light Brown + 1 pump Graphite
= 4 pumps dye total
+ 4 pumps developer
= ready to apply
The Rule — Every Time
Step 1: Mix your dye shades
Step 2: Count total dye pumps
Step 3: Add that exact number in developer
Dye pumps = Developer pumps. Always.
⚠  Once developer is added, your mixture is active. Use within 10 minutes and never store leftover activated product. Mix dye shades first — developer always goes in last.

Blending Rules
Mix Order
Always mix dye shades together first, then add developer. Never mix developer with one shade then add another dye.
Start Light
Add darker shades drop-by-drop. Easy to darken a blend but impossible to lighten once mixed.
Time Sensitivity
Once developer is added, use within 10 minutes. Never store after activation.
Patch Test
Always perform a patch test 48hrs prior — applies to blended formulas too.
